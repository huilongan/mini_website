Introduction

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

A site for visualizing history reviews,
recommending restaurants for those yelp users

The yelp review system mainly serves for those YELP USERS who are interested in their past review patterns, eating habits, preferance on restaurants. We also find BEST taste and recommend RIGHT restaurants for users by analyzing their historical reviews for different restaurants. The dataset we used are all provided by Yelp Data Challenge.
